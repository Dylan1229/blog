This is the first post on my blog. I plan to write about ML systems, GPU computing, research experiences, and things I find interesting along the way.

## Why a blog?

Papers capture finished ideas, but the messy middle — debugging CUDA kernels at 2 AM, figuring out why a distributed training job hangs, choosing between competing system designs — rarely gets written down. This blog is for that.

## What to expect

- Notes on ML system optimization and efficient inference
- Paper reading notes and conference experiences
- Occasional life updates

Stay tuned.
